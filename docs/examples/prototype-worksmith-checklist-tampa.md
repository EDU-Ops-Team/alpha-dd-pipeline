## 3. Site-Specific Tasks

These items were flagged by AI research as needing field verification for this specific property. They go beyond the standard checklist.

| # | Task | Why It Matters | What to Document |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Verify NO basement or below-grade space exists | 1955 Florida building — basements uncommon but not impossible. Basement = mandatory sprinklers ($30K–$50K) + 6–8 weeks. This is a deal-killer. | Walk entire building. Check for any below-grade access (stairs down, sloped entries). Photograph foundation perimeter. |
| 2 | Measure exit separation distance | IBC requires exits separated by ≥ 1/3 of max diagonal. At 3,430 SF single-story, diagonal is ~80–100 ft → exits must be ≥23–33 ft apart. | Measure distance between the two farthest exit doors. If <23 ft, note as deficiency. |
| 3 | Assess kitchen/food prep infrastructure | FL DOH (FAC 6A-2.0040) requires handwashing and food safety capability for K-12. | 4 | Document construction type | Cannot be determined remotely for a 1955 building.
